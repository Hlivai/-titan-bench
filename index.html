Understanding The TITAN v6.0 Algorithm

TITAN v6.0 is not just a standard pricing calculator. It is a comprehensive SaaS platform built for gamers, tech enthusiasts, and hardware resellers. Whether you are trying to find the resale value of an old iPhone, checking if your Custom PC has a CPU bottleneck, or analyzing battery degradation, our 150-million operation web worker ensures extreme accuracy.

Live Bottleneck Detection (PC)

Building a custom gaming rig requires balance. If you pair a legacy Intel Core i7-4790K with a modern NVIDIA RTX 4070, your graphics card will be starved for data. TITAN detects this price-to-performance disparity and issues a Red Alert to prevent you from making a bad purchase.

Mobile Battery & OS Diagnostics

Phones degrade differently than PCs. An iPhone X might look brand new, but internally, its battery chemical age is severely depleted, and Apple has ceased major iOS updates for it. TITAN calculates the exact release year of the device and warns buyers if the phone is entering End-of-Life (EOL).

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the hardware prices?

Prices are modeled based on current US and EU used marketplaces (like eBay, Swappa, and StockX). We update the baseline algorithm to reflect natural tech depreciation.

What does the "Hardware Stress Benchmark" do?

When you click "Execute Deep Appraisal", your browser runs a Javascript Web Worker that performs 150 million floating-point math operations. This checks your CPU's actual logical thread speed independently of theoretical specs.

Why did I get an OS Obsolescence Warning on my phone?

If you select a phone released over 4-5 years ago, our system flags it. Old phones lose app support (like WhatsApp or banking apps) and usually require immediate battery replacements, which heavily lowers their cash value.
