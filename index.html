Why Benchmark Your CPU?

Benchmarking is essential for gamers, developers, and tech enthusiasts. It helps you understand if your processor is thermal throttling or if background apps are draining your resources. Titan Bench uses high-precision math calculations to push your CPU to its limits.

Multi-Core Performance

Modern CPUs have multiple cores. Our tool utilizes Web Workers to engage all available threads simultaneously, giving you a comprehensive score of your multi-tasking capabilities.

Real-Time Processing

Unlike static tests, Titan Bench calculates square roots and trigonometric functions in real-time, simulating heavy tasks like video rendering or AI processing.
