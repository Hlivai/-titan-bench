Privacy Policy

At Titan Valuation, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your device data during the valuation process.

1. Data Collection

To provide an accurate price estimate, our tool accesses basic hardware specifications such as CPU cores and performance metrics via the browser. We do not access personal files, photos, or private data.

2. Cookies and Ads

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements. Google may use DART cookies to show ads based on your visit to our site and other sites on the internet. You can opt out of this by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

3. Use of Estimates

The performance data is processed locally on your device to generate a valuation score. This data is not stored on our servers.
